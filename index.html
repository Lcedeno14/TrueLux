About

True Lux Construction was built on a simple standard: projects should be executed with clarity, discipline, and care from start to finish.

We approach each project with careful planning, professional oversight, and attention to detail — prioritizing coordination, accountability, and clean execution at every stage. The goal is always the same: a well-managed process and a finished result that holds up over time.

Our work includes residential construction and renovation, custom carpentry, outdoor living environments, and select commercial projects. We regularly work alongside homeowners, real estate agents, and property management teams on projects that require discretion, organization, and dependable follow-through.

True Lux Construction is a licensed and insured California general contractor serving Los Angeles and the surrounding areas. The company is led by licensed construction professionals with experience managing complex scopes and coordinating multi-trade projects.

Process

A clear, structured approach designed to keep projects organized, transparent, and well-executed from start to finish.

Consultation

Every project begins with a focused discussion to understand scope, priorities, and expectations. This allows us to assess the project and determine the most effective path forward.

Planning & Proposal

We develop a clear, well-defined plan outlining scope, schedule, and budget considerations. Proposals are prepared with clarity to support informed decision-making and smooth execution.

Execution

Projects are managed with professional oversight, consistent communication, and attention to detail. We coordinate trades, maintain an organized job site, and keep work progressing efficiently.

Completion

Upon completion, we conduct a final walkthrough to ensure all work meets expectations and project objectives are fully delivered.
