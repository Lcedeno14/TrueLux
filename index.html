About

True Lux Construction was built on a simple standard: projects should be well planned, properly managed, and executed with care from start to finish.

We approach each project with a focus on organization, accountability, and consistent oversight. From early planning through completion, we prioritize coordination and execution so work progresses smoothly and expectations remain aligned.

Our work includes residential and multifamily construction and renovation, building-wide improvements, and select commercial projects. We manage projects of varying size and complexity, providing structured oversight across all phases of work.

True Lux Construction works with homeowners, owners, real estate professionals, and project teams, providing responsive communication, reliable execution, and close attention to detail throughout each project.
